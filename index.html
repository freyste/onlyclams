◈ WHAT IS ONLYCLAMS?

OnlyClams is a free weekly email alert for clam diggers on the Northern California coast. Every Wednesday morning it automatically checks tide predictions, weather forecasts, and daylight hours for the upcoming weekend — and emails you only when conditions are actually worth suiting up for.

No app. No subscription fee. No endless scrolling through tide charts. Just a clean forecast straight to your inbox when the clams are calling. 🦪

◈ HOW WE PICK A GREAT DAY
🗓️ DAY OF WEEK Friday, Saturday, Sunday, or Monday only We skip the work week — weekends are for clamming.
🌡️ WEATHER Daily high temperature ≥ 55°F Life's too short to dig clams in the cold.
🌊 TIDE At least one low tide below 0.0 ft MLLW between sunrise and sunset Negative daytime tides only — that's when the good stuff is exposed.
